about me

I'm someone who finds connection in the spaces between things — between people, between systems, between the digital and the everyday. Networking, to me, isn't just technical; it's about building something that holds.

When I'm not thinking about infrastructure and how things communicate, I'm usually deep in a game — the kind that asks something of you. Strategy, story, the long play. Gaming has always been a way I make sense of complex systems, and I've never stopped finding it fascinating.

And then there are the dogs. Loyal, honest, and entirely uncomplicated. They remind me what actually matters.
